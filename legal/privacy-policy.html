Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

GolfPrice AI ("we", "us", "our") operates golfpriceai.com. This page explains what information we collect, why, and the choices you have. We've written it in plain language rather than dense legal text.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you sign up for deal alerts. We use this to send you the emails you asked for, and nothing else.
  • Usage data — standard, non-identifying information like which pages you visit and which browser you're using, collected automatically to help us understand what's useful and fix what isn't.
  • Cookies — small files stored in your browser. See our Cookie Policy for the full breakdown of what we use and why.

How we use it

  • To send deal alert emails to people who've signed up for them
  • To understand which pages and deals people find useful, so we can improve the site
  • To keep the site secure and working properly

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Affiliate links

GolfPrice AI is reader-supported. When you click a product link on this site and make a purchase, we may earn a commission from the retailer at no extra cost to you. As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases. This doesn't affect the price you pay, and it doesn't influence which deals we choose to feature — see our affiliate disclosure for more detail.

Email service provider

If you sign up for deal alerts, your email address is stored with a third-party email service provider (such as Mailchimp or beehiiv) solely to send you those alerts. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

Your rights

If you're in the UK or EU, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, and the right to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these, email us at the address below.

Data retention

We keep your email address for as long as you're subscribed to deal alerts. If you unsubscribe, it's removed from our active mailing list.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
