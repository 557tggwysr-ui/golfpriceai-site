🏌️ Why GolfPrice AI Exists

Buying golf gear shouldn't feel like guesswork.

Prices jump around constantly, "limited-time deals" aren't always deals, and somehow your playing partner always swears they paid less.

GolfPrice AI was built to make buying golf gear simpler, smarter, and far less painful.

We track prices, spot genuine bargains, and help golfers avoid spending driver money on what should've been a 7-iron purchase.

No hype. No fake discounts. Just better golf gear prices.

How this actually started

It began with a fairly ordinary frustration: trying to work out whether a driver "deal" was actually a deal, or just a normal price wearing a red sticker.

That meant five browser tabs, three retailer sites, and a Sunday afternoon that could've been spent, well, actually playing golf.

So GolfPrice AI does the boring bit — checking prices, constantly — so you don't have to lose an afternoon to a spreadsheet just to buy a wedge.

The honest bit

GolfPrice AI is a small, independently-run project — not a giant retailer, not owned by any golf brand, and not backed by anyone with a vested interest in which driver you buy.

The catalog and retailer partnerships are growing every week. Some deals are still being tracked manually while that happens — but every single one is checked, not guessed.
